Included Usage & Voice Policy

The commercial promise is subscriptions plus prepaid voice packs, not open-ended public metering.

Solo

$79 / month

120 included live voice minutes, then expansion through the 250-minute prepaid pack.

Team

$50 / seat / month

3-seat minimum with 120 pooled live voice minutes per seat and prepaid team packs for overage.

Annual

10% prepaid discount

Annual billing improves cash collection and fee efficiency without changing the public menu.

  • Solo includes 120 live voice minutes each month and expands through a 250-minute pack.
  • Team includes 120 live minutes per seat, pooled at the org level, with 500- and 2000-minute packs for overage.
  • Internal per-minute underwriting rates stay in the appendix; the public packaging stays on subscriptions and packs.

Base Usage Assumptions

The model uses explicit inputs for voice, scored calls, and shared software floor rather than hidden averages.

Live voice model
$0.08 / min

Internal planning conversion from OpenAI realtime token pricing, not an official vendor minute quote.

Scored call model
$0.325 / call

52.5 transcription minutes plus a small GPT-5 mini scoring buffer, with 4 calls per seat monthly.

Shared software floor
$173.83 / mo

Vercel, Supabase, Fly, HighLevel, and Resend spread across 25 active paying orgs in the base case.

  • Live voice is modeled at $0.08 per minute; this is an internal conversion from OpenAI realtime token pricing, not an official minute quote.
  • Scored calls are modeled at $0.325 per 45-60 minute call, using 52.5 minutes of transcription plus a small GPT-5 mini scoring buffer.
  • Base usage assumes 4 scored calls per seat per month and spreads the $173.83 software floor across 25 active paying orgs.
  • Optional gpt-4o-mini-tts playback is ~$0.015 per minute and is excluded from base live voice metering.

Closing Thesis

The pricing system stays simple, the margin structure is readable, and live voice is monetized rather than subsidized.

The pricing system stays simple, the margin structure is readable, and live voice is monetized rather than subsidized.

  • Two subscription plans cover the menu: Solo for individuals and Team with a hard three-seat floor.
  • Product gross margins remain strong under full included voice assumptions.
  • Stripe drag is visible and manageable rather than hidden.
  • Voice expansion is bounded, pooled, and monetized through prepaid packs.
